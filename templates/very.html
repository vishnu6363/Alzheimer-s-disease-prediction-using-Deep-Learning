Alzhimer disease prediction

The Stage is -Very Mild Demented

You still might not notice anything amiss in your loved one's behavior, but they may be picking up on small differences, things that even a doctor doesn't catch.
This could include forgetting words or misplacing objects.
At this stage, subtle symptoms of Alzheimer's don't interfere with their ability to work or live independently.
Keep in mind that these symptoms might not be Alzheimer's at all, but simply normal changes from aging.
